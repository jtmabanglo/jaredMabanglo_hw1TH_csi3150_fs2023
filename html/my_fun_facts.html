Some Fun Facts About Me:

Favorite Book:

New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

The Bible is my favorite book because the wisdom contained in it has helped me in many aspects of my life. This translation in particular is my favorite because it uses modern English that is easy to understand while at the same time remaining faithful to the original text. I try to read at least a small portion of it each day.

You can access it for free on JW.ORG.
